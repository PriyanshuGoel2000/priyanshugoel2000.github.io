Box Shooter

A FIRST PERSON BOX SHOOTER GAME DEVELOPED IN UNITY 3D..

Box Shooter is a Game Developed in Unity. It is a first person perspective game where player has to shoot the boxes to collect points.

INSTRUCTIONS:
The game consists of different boxes(currently 3) that player has to shoot. The Green Box will gain you points. Yellow box will gain you time. White box will deduct your points and time.
The player can shoot the boxes in the level which increases their points. The boxes appear for a particular time only. There are Green Boxes in very great number and white and yellow are in very less number to appear.
The 3D space is enclosed and you can only stand on the platform. Hovering in the air may help.

Controls:
Up Arrow key or 'W' to Move Player Forward.
Down Arrow key or 'S' to Move Player Backwards.
Left Arrow key or 'A' to Move Player Left.
Right Arrow key or 'D' to Move Player Right.
Press and Hold Space Bar to Hover.
