Solar System Simulation

A Simulation showing the Solar System and the Planets in it.

Solar System Simulation is a simulation of the Solar System. Showing different planets in our Solar System such as Mercury, Earth, Venus, etc.

INSTRUCTIONS:
The Simulation consists of different planets that the player can focus on by clicking on them.
A planet can be focused by clicking on them in the big screen or the top view screen in the bottom left corner.

Controls:
Left Mouse Button to Click on the Planets.
