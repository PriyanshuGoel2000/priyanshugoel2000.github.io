SUPER SPARTY BROs

SUPER SPARTY BRO IS A FINAL PROJECT GAME MADE AS A PROJECT FOR THE GAME DEVELOPMENT FOR MODERN PLATFORMS COURSE OF THE MICHIGAN STATE UNIVERSITY

Super Sparty Bros is a recreation of a game based on characters Sparty Bros. just like Mario bros. It is a project that was to be developed for the online course "Game Development for Modern Platforms" of the Michigan State University available on coursera.

INSTRUCTIONS:
The game consists of different levels(currently 3) that player has to pass. The level consists of a Rose that the player has to collect in order to go to next level.
The player can collect the coins in the level which increases their points by one per each coin. The coins are being guarded by enemy guards so player must be aware of them or they will kill you. You can stun the enemy guard by hitting or jumping on their head this stuns them for a short period of time.
There are some terrains player must not go into such as lava and the waterfall. The pointed brick is also dangerous.

Controls:
Left Arrow key or 'A' to Move Player Left.
Right Arrow key or 'D' to Move Player Right.
Space Bar to Jump.
