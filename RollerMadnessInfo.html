Roller Madness

ROLLER MADNESS, A 3D GAME PROJECT BUILT IN UNITY3D. YOU PLAY AS A BALL, MOVE IT TO COLLECT COINS AND AVOID ENEMIES.

Roller Madness is one of the 3D games i was really excited to make.It is an arcade based game. In this game the player is a ball and the player has to collect coins while staying within the boundaries of the platform. The game gets challenging when the emenies show up to kill you. Save yourself from enemies(Spoiler alert: the cubes are the enemies.) and collect as much coins as you can.

INSTRUCTIONS:
The game consists of different levels(currently 2) that player has to collect coins.
The player has to collect coins dropping from above in the level which increases their points. The boxes are the enemies which are trying to catch you, so you must be safe from them.
The player can only move on the platform, other is danger ground and the player will die if they go outside of the platform. Keep the Drag in mind.

Controls:
Up Arrow key or 'W' to Move Player Forward.
Down Arrow key or 'S' to Move Player Backwards.
Left Arrow key or 'A' to Move Player Left.
Right Arrow key or 'D' to Move Player Right.
